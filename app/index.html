New features 4.0

Looking Sharp

Our focus, when redesigning our User Interface, was keeping our Content Management System powerful yet fun & easy to use. We are proud to release n-cast 4.0 with a look & feel that appeals to all types of users, both technical and not. Including Drag-and-drop, generating thumbnails and letting you search, sort and tag all content. Built browser friendly with responsive design, n-cast will lead the way to the Future of Digital Signage.

Redefining borders

A new dimension in content Scheduling. N-cast introduces integrated GeoFence technology. Targeting location based content has never been so easy. Just select a position on the map and draw a radius. Every time the GPS of a player detects that it accesses a GeoFence, the targeted campaign will be shown on the screens instantly. In-vehicle Digital Signage, let’s move.

Adapting new Display Technology

N-cast has developed a SoC version of its player technology to support the new LG WebOS for Signage platform. The WebOS player is HTML5 based and supports different types of content including full-screen and multi-zone playback. WebOS for Signage eliminates the need for an external media player simplifying and reducing installation, operational and energy costs. Only requires a power and (wireless) LAN connection.

Fuel & Donate

“Fuel up & Donate” is a brand new loyalty concept at gas stations in the Netherlands. For every liter gasoline you earn a point, that you can donate to a charity or organization that you would like to support. You can select a charity at the tablet on the counter, and your points will be shown on the donation board. The donation board is powered by n-cast digital signage software and runs on WebOS. Find out more on the fast growing network of Gas Stations at www.tankenschenk.nl
