n-cast

Software

n-cast is a scalable cloud-based Content Management System for Digital Signage networks. Simple, safe, good looking & for every type of industry. We are proud to release n-cast 4.0 with a look & feel that appeals to all types of users, both technical and not.

By using n-cast you can create Digital Signage Networks in any size or scale. Multiple users, with their own rights and roles can work in virtual groups creating stunning Template based content in multi zone lay-outs. Let us introduce you to the Future of Digital Signage. Just leave your contact details and we will contact you for an (online) demo.

Key features

Browser friendly, Responsive design, Skinnable, Scalable, Multi-lingual, Secure, Integrated workflow management, HTML5, Drag & drop, E-mail notification, Live feeds & social media integration, Template support, Integrated photo editor, Thumbnail generator.

Content Management

Easily manage all your content by creating your own library. Upload media assets, videos, images & logo’s from your desktop. Drag & Drop files on the fly. Create new media by using powerful templates with eye-catching animations.

Include live feeds News, Weather, Traffic info and Social Media like Facebook, Twitter & Instagram. Fullscreen or as a tickertape.

n-cast will generate thumbnails for you and let you search, sort and tag all content. Our integrated photo editor will help you crop, flip & adjust your images just the way you want.

Publishing

Our build-in Campaign wizard will help you schedule your content in just a few easy steps. Drag & drop media in your campaign and target it at the right date, time & group. Add segments like lunch, dinner or any other day-parting you need.

Besides scheduling a campaign it is also possible to trigger campaigns based on a Geo-Fence, External trigger or let it show exclusively.

Playlists are generated, based on campaigns, and can display full screen or within a multi-zone lay-out.

Publishing new content can be done by multiple users, at different levels all based on customizable roles.

Workspaces

Having different workspaces makes you feel comfortable, when dealing with larger Digital Signage Networks. Within each workspace you can set-up roles, define hierarchy and edit preferences. Unleash the power of creating virtual groups for any type of scheduling. Each workspace can be ‘skinned’ to be in sync with your customer’s identity.

Network

n-cast is not only about content management. It allows you to monitor and organize your network of screens and players too.

You will know exactly where your hardware is located, how to contact a technical or commercial person on site and set rules for turning on and of the screens with advanced display controller functionality.

n-cast can monitor and alert via e-mail notification on different user levels. With no additional burden for your IT department. Secure, save and very flexible.

Reporting

With the powerful reporting tool you can extract all information you need. There are various reports on playback, usage, and network status based on time, date, media, campaign or group. Reports can be exported in PDF and Excel format for your convenience. Our Software development team is eager to customize reports, to meet your needs.
